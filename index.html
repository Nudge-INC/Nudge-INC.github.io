GPS Alarm Functionality

Fell asleep and missed your stop? Say no more! With the Nudge app, you can pre-program your bus route and get the app to wake you up at your destination.

Nudge's GPS alarm clock gives you the ability to set your route on the app and will notify you on approach. It can be used as a general GPS to give you the recommended routes that will take you to your destination in the fastest and easiest way possible. Nudge's most popular feature wakes you up if you dozed off and you're nearing your stop! Perfect for everyday commuters.

Speedometer

You don't have to wait for New Year's Day to start exercising. Enhance your fitness routine with Nudge's speedometer function!

Getting fit just got easier with Nudge's built-in speedometer! Track your speed while running, biking or any other physical activity with your phone or smartwatch.

Standard GPS Function

Forgot your map at home? No worries! You can use the built-in GPS function to find the quickest route to your destination.

The GPS function in the Nudge app is the best option to help you reach your destination in a pinch. With over 100 different routes preplanned for various cities, Nudge will surely find you the most effective way to get you places.
